How this list was built

Starting from the full 535-session Snowflake Summit '26 catalog, every session was scored against four signals. A session was kept if any of these matched, and prioritized based on which:

  • RAI account on the schedule — from the prospect, customer, and partner list shared in the May 22 prep call.
  • Direct topic overlap with RAI positioning — ontology, semantic layer/views, knowledge graph, decision intelligence, reasoning, eval, super-alignment, Open Semantic Interchange.
  • Strategic narratives — Context Layer, Agentic Enterprise, "Future of [Industry]" panels, founders/VCs sessions — the rooms where the category story is being written.
  • Adjacent agentic/GenAI — only included when intersected with a vertical RAI plays in (Telco, FinServ, Manufacturing, HCLS, Retail) or a vendor we want to watch (Atlan, Workday, Hex, ThoughtSpot, etc.). Generic agent sessions were filtered out as noise.
